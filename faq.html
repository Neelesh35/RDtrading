Frequently asked questions

Quick answers for delivery, stock and payment.

Do you deliver across Mauritius?

Yes. Standard and express delivery options are shown at checkout.

Can I order on WhatsApp?

Yes. There are WhatsApp buttons across the site for direct support and assisted checkout.

How do I know if something is in stock?

Stock is shown on product cards and product pages.

What payment methods are available?

Cash on Delivery, Bank Transfer, Juice by MCB, and Visa / Mastercard.
